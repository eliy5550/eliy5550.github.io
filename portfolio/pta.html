About This Game

LINK FOR GOOGLE PLAY PAGE
Poop Them All is my first release on GooglePlay.
It got over 100 downloads.

You play as a bird and you fly above a park. Pedestrians are walking by and your objective is to poop on their heads.
The game is quite challenging because you can't just poop on the floor and you can't let pedestrians escape.
So, you can miss or waste poop only 3 times.
Every time you hit someone with your poop, you earn points and the game saves your highest score, is also gives you in game currency.

With the currency you can buy abilities such as more lives or lowering the bird's height.
You can also buy a secret bird.
I implemented Unity's advertisments system, the game shows an ad every 5 games.
