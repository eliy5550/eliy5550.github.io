About This Game

LINK FOR STEAM PAGE
JEFF-16 is my first release on Steam. It's my biggest game and I'm selling it for 6 dollars for 1 unit.

It's an obstacle course, flying shooter game. You play as Jeff the F-16 pilot and you fly through glowing golden hoops while shooting enemy cars and choppers. In the forth level you also drop supplies on buildings. At the end you fight a giant skyscraper as the final boss. As a weapon you have homing missiles that will destroy enemies in one shot. I programmed everything.

JEFF-16 has 5 big levels with beautiful 3D graphics. I designed all the levels with assets from the Unity Asset Store and formed the terrain with Unity Terrain. I also added post processing and effects.

The game incluses a small story about a pilot who's city got attacked by an army and avenges by destroy the them at their territory.
